Help

How to create an account?

To create an account, click on the 'Sign up' button on the home page. Fill out the required information
such as your email, username, and password, then click 'Sign Up' to complete the process.

How to access tests?

To access tests, log in to your account and navigate to the 'Tests' section. You should be able to see a list of available tests. Click on the test you want to take to begin.

How many tests do we need to get recommendations?

The candidate should take at least 3 tests to capture the insights. However, the number of tests required to receive recommendations may vary depending on the platform's algorithm and your activity. Generally, the more tests you take, the better the recommendations become as the system learns more about your preferences and abilities.

Can we have domain-based tests?

Yes, we offer domain-based tests in various subjects and areas of expertise. You can explore different categories or domains to find tests that match your interests or professional requirements.
